# Project Catalog

Anchor projects by season and year-round theme. Each entry: name, age band, supplies, prep/activity time, mess, skill level, learning connection, safety flags, one-line summary. Use as inspiration and calibration — novel themes may generate fresh projects, but the shape and constraints should match these anchors.

## Fall

### Torn-Paper Leaf Collage — 2-4
Supplies: construction paper (white + fall colors), glue stick. 5/15 min. Low mess. Beginner. Fine motor, color recognition, seasonal vocab. No flags. _Tear fall-color paper and press onto a leaf outline._

### Handprint Turkey — 2-4
Supplies: construction paper (brown + fall colors), washable paint OR markers, glue stick. 5/20 min. Medium mess (paint). Beginner. Body awareness, counting (5 fingers = 5 feathers), Thanksgiving vocab. Flag: paint smocks. _Trace or paint the child's hand; each finger is a feather._

### Apple Stamping — 2-4
Supplies: real apple halves, red/yellow/green washable paint, white paper. 5/15 min. Medium-high mess. Beginner. Pattern-making, color mixing, seasonal vocab. Flag: paint smocks. _Dip apple halves in paint and stamp starburst shapes._

### Pumpkin Seed Mosaic — 5-7
Supplies: construction paper pumpkin cutout, cleaned pumpkin seeds, white glue, orange marker. 10/25 min. Low mess. Beginner. Fine motor, texture. Flag: small parts supervised. _Glue seeds onto a pumpkin shape like scales on a fish._

### Paper-Plate Scarecrow — 5-7
Supplies: paper plate, yarn, construction-paper scraps, glue stick, markers, raffia or yellow yarn. 10/25 min. Low mess. Beginner. Fine motor, storytelling. _Glue yarn "straw" around a paper-plate face._

### Fall Leaf Rubbings — 5-7
Supplies: real leaves, white paper, peeled crayons. 5/15 min. Low mess. Beginner. Science (leaf veins), texture. _Place leaves under paper and rub crayon sideways to reveal veins._

### Cardboard Tree with Pressed Leaves — 8-10
Supplies: cardboard, scissors, real pressed leaves, white glue, brown paint. 15/35 min. Medium mess. Intermediate. Science, sustainability. _Cut a tree silhouette and glue pressed leaves as the canopy._

### Origami Pumpkin — 8-10
Supplies: orange square paper, green paper strip, glue stick. 0/25 min. Low mess. Intermediate. Geometry, sequencing. _Fold a traditional origami pumpkin and add a green stem._

### Woven Paper Pumpkin Placemat — 8-10
Supplies: orange and yellow construction paper, scissors, glue stick. 10/30 min. Low mess. Intermediate. Pattern recognition, bilateral coordination. _Weave orange strips through a slitted yellow base._

### Spider-Web Yarn Art (Halloween-generic) — 5-7
Supplies: black paper plate, white yarn, hole punch, small plastic spider or pom-pom. 10/25 min. Low mess. Beginner. Fine motor, geometry (radial symmetry). _Wrap yarn across punched holes to form a web._

### Candy-Corn Paper Strip Craft — 5-7
Supplies: yellow/orange/white construction paper strips, glue stick. 5/15 min. Low mess. Beginner. Color sequencing, fine motor. _Stack three color strips into a candy-corn wedge._

### Scented Apple-Pie Playdough — 2-4
Supplies: flour, salt, cream of tartar, water, cinnamon, nutmeg. 15/20 min play. Medium mess. Beginner. Sensory, measuring. Flag: non-edible (high salt); smocks. _Cook cinnamon playdough on stovetop (adult) and sculpt apples._

### Thanksgiving Gratitude Leaves — 5-10
Supplies: construction paper leaves (pre-cut or templated), markers, hole punch, yarn. 10/20 min. Low mess. Beginner. SEL, literacy. _Write one thing each child is grateful for on a paper leaf._

### Corn-Husk Doll (simplified) — 8-10
Supplies: dried corn husks, twine, scissors, bowl of warm water. 15/40 min. Medium mess (wet husks). Intermediate. Cultural awareness, fine motor. _Soak, tie, and shape husks into a simple figure._

### Painted Pinecone Creatures — 5-7
Supplies: pinecones, washable paint, googly eyes (1"+), tacky glue, pipe cleaners. 5/25 min. Medium mess. Beginner. Nature, imagination. Flag: small parts (≥1" googly). _Paint pinecones and add eyes to make woodland creatures._

## Winter

### Coffee-Filter Snowflakes — 2-4
Supplies: coffee filters, washable markers, spray bottle with water. 5/20 min. Low-medium mess. Beginner. Science (dispersion), symmetry. _Draw with markers on a coffee filter, then mist to spread color._

### Paper-Plate Snowman — 2-4
Supplies: paper plate, cotton balls, glue stick, construction paper scraps (orange nose, black hat). 5/20 min. Low mess. Beginner. Fine motor, winter vocab. _Glue cotton balls across a paper plate; add face pieces._

### Folded Paper Snowflakes — 5-7
Supplies: white printer paper (square), kid scissors. 5/20 min. Low mess. Beginner. Symmetry, fine motor. Flag: scissors 5+. _Fold paper in sixths and snip shapes; unfold._

### Hand-Shaped Menorah — 5-7
Supplies: blue construction paper, yellow/orange paper, glue stick, marker. 5/20 min. Low mess. Beginner. Cultural awareness, counting. _Trace two hands thumbs-together; each finger is a candle._

### Paper-Strip Ornament Ball — 5-7
Supplies: construction paper strips, stapler (adult) or brass fastener, ribbon. 5/20 min. Low mess. Beginner. 3D construction. _Fan five strips and connect the ends to make a ball._

### Salt-Painted Snowflake — 5-7
Supplies: white glue, table salt, watercolor paints, dark cardstock. 10/25 min + dry. Medium mess. Intermediate. Science (capillary action). _Draw a snowflake in glue, cover in salt, drop watercolor to travel the salt._

### Paper Snow Globe — 5-7
Supplies: paper plate (2 per child), plastic wrap OR wax paper, markers, paper scraps, glue stick. 10/30 min. Low mess. Intermediate. Creativity. _Cut a window in a plate, tape plastic across, and sandwich a scene inside._

### Origami Dreidel — 8-10
Supplies: square blue paper, marker. 0/20 min. Low mess. Intermediate. Geometry, cultural awareness. _Fold a traditional origami dreidel and label the four Hebrew letters._

### Bird-Seed Ornaments — 5-10
Supplies: bird seed, gelatin, cookie cutters, twine. 20 prep (adult) / 20 assemble. Medium mess. Beginner. Science, environmental. Flag: adult for hot gelatin. _Mold bird-seed-and-gelatin mix in cookie cutters to hang outdoors._

### Kwanzaa Mkeka Woven Placemat — 5-10
Supplies: red, black, green construction paper, glue stick, scissors. 10/30 min. Low mess. Intermediate. Cultural awareness, pattern. _Weave red and green strips through a black base._

### Lunar New Year Paper Lantern — 5-10
Supplies: red construction paper, scissors, glue stick, gold marker or yarn. 5/25 min. Low mess. Beginner-intermediate. Cultural awareness, geometry. _Fold red paper, make slit cuts, then roll into a lantern tube._

### Valentine Heart-Weaving — 5-7
Supplies: red and pink cardstock, scissors, glue stick. 10/30 min. Low mess. Intermediate. Symmetry, pattern. Flag: scissors 5+. _Cut curved slits and weave two hearts into one._

### Valentine Mailbox (Classroom) — 5-10
Supplies: shoeboxes or paper bags, construction paper, markers, stickers, tape. 10 prep / 30 activity. Low mess. Beginner. SEL, literacy. _Decorate a personal mailbox for friendship notes._

### Winter Animal Track Stamps — 5-7
Supplies: foam sheet, scissors, washable paint, white paper. 15 prep (adult cuts) / 20 activity. Medium mess. Beginner. Science (animal signs). _Stamp foam "tracks" across a snowy scene._

### Paper-Quill Snowflake — 8-10
Supplies: paper quilling strips or cut-down cardstock, glue, pencil for rolling. 0/35 min. Low mess. Advanced. Fine motor, geometry. _Roll and shape paper strips into snowflake patterns._

## Spring

### Coffee-Filter Butterfly — 2-4
Supplies: coffee filter, washable markers, spray bottle, pipe cleaner. 5/20 min. Low-medium mess. Beginner. Science (dispersion), symmetry. _Color, spritz, pinch the middle, and twist with a pipe cleaner._

### Cotton-Ball Lamb — 2-4
Supplies: cotton balls, black construction paper (pre-cut lamb body), glue stick, googly eyes (1"+). 5/15 min. Low mess. Beginner. Texture, animal vocab. Flag: large googly. _Glue cotton across a lamb silhouette._

### Paper-Plate Flower — 2-4
Supplies: paper plate (pre-snipped petals), washable paint, yellow center cutout. 10 prep / 20 activity. Medium mess. Beginner. Color mixing. _Paint petals and glue a yellow center._

### Handprint Tulip Card — 2-4
Supplies: green and pink/yellow construction paper, glue stick, marker. 5/15 min. Low mess. Beginner. Gift-making, Mother's/Father's Day. _Trace the child's hand; fold fingers inward to make a tulip._

### Seed-Sprouting Cup — 5-7
Supplies: clear plastic cup, cotton balls, bean or grass seeds, water, marker. 5/15 min + watch daily. Low mess. Beginner. Science (germination). _Press damp cotton into a cup, nestle seeds against the side, water daily._

### Egg-Carton Flower Bouquet — 5-7
Supplies: egg carton, scissors (adult pre-cuts), washable paint, pipe cleaners, green paper. 10 prep / 25 activity. Medium mess. Beginner. Upcycling, color mixing. _Paint egg-carton cups as blossoms and attach pipe-cleaner stems._

### Tissue-Paper Stained-Glass Butterfly — 5-7
Supplies: wax paper, tissue-paper scraps, glue stick, black construction paper frame. 10/25 min. Low mess. Intermediate. Symmetry, color. _Layer tissue scraps between wax paper and frame with a cut butterfly outline._

### Paper Rain Stick — 5-7
Supplies: paper towel tube, dry rice or beans, aluminum foil, tape, markers. 10/25 min. Medium mess. Beginner. Sound science. Flag: small parts if under 3. _Crumple foil into the tube, pour rice, seal ends, decorate._

### Easter Egg Paper Mosaic — 5-7
Supplies: construction paper (pre-cut squares), egg-shape cardstock, glue stick. 10 prep / 20 activity. Low mess. Beginner. Pattern, fine motor. _Tile small squares onto an egg shape._

### Bird's Nest Diorama — 5-7
Supplies: brown paper bag, small twigs, shredded paper or raffia, pom-poms (1"+), glue. 10/30 min. Medium mess. Intermediate. Science (nest-building). _Cut and flatten a bag as a nest base; glue twigs and pom-pom eggs._

### Mother's/Father's Day Handprint Keepsake — 5-7
Supplies: white cardstock, washable paint, marker. 5/20 min. Medium mess. Beginner. Gift-making. _Paint the child's hand and press onto cardstock with a poem._

### Earth-Day Recycled Robot — 5-7
Supplies: cereal boxes, bottle caps, tin-foil scraps, tape, markers. 0/30 min. Low mess. Beginner. Environmental awareness, 3D assembly. _Tape a robot body from box pieces; bottle caps become eyes and buttons._

### Earth-Day Recycled Bird Feeder — 8-10
Supplies: toilet-paper tubes, peanut or sunflower butter (allergy flag), bird seed, string. 5/20 min. Medium mess. Beginner. Environmental awareness. Flag: peanut allergen — default sunflower butter. _Coat a tube in butter, roll in seed, and hang outdoors._

### Origami Tulip — 8-10
Supplies: square pink/red paper, green paper strip, glue. 0/25 min. Low mess. Intermediate. Geometry. _Fold a traditional origami tulip and mount on a paper stem._

### Watercolor Rain Clouds — 5-10
Supplies: watercolor paper, cotton balls, blue watercolor, droppers, liquid glue. 5/25 min + dry. Medium mess. Intermediate. Science (rain), color. _Glue cotton clouds; drip blue watercolor to "rain" below._

### Papier-Mâché Planet Earth — 8-10
Supplies: balloon, newspaper strips, flour-and-water paste, blue and green paint. 20/40 min (+ 24h dry). High mess. Advanced. Environmental awareness, geography. Flag: drying time; wheat in paste. _Layer strips on a balloon, dry, paint continents._

Supplies: yellow/orange paint, construction paper. 5/15 min. Medium mess. Beginner. Seasonal vocab. _Press painted handprints in a circle to form sun rays._

### Coffee-Filter Jellyfish — 2-4

Supplies: blue cardstock, white glue, sand OR uncooked oats, construction-paper shell shapes. 5/20 min + dry. Medium mess. Beginner. Texture, ocean. _Draw a beach line in glue and sprinkle sand._

### Paper-Plate Watermelon — 2-4
Supplies: paper plate (half), pink and green paint OR paper, glue stick, black marker. 5/15 min. Medium mess. Beginner. Fruit vocab. _Paint a half-plate pink with green rind; dot black seeds._

### Egg-Carton Caterpillar — 2-4
Supplies: egg carton (row of 4-6 cups), washable paint, pipe cleaners, googly eyes (1"+). 5 prep / 20 activity. Medium mess. Beginner. Upcycling, bug vocab. _Paint a row of egg cups; add pipe-cleaner antennae._

### Paper Camping Scene Diorama — 8-10
Supplies: shoebox or cereal box, construction paper, markers, small twigs, red/yellow tissue paper (fire). 10/40 min. Low mess. Intermediate. Storytelling. _Build a tent and campfire scene inside an open box._

### Firework Straw Painting — 5-7

### Fourth-of-July Paper Fan — 5-7
Supplies: red, white, blue paper, glue stick, tongue depressor or chopstick. 5/20 min. Low mess. Beginner. Color/flag vocab. _Accordion-fold paper and secure to a stick._

### Garden-Stone Painting — 8-10
Supplies: smooth rocks, acrylic paint (age flag), brushes, sealant (optional, adult). 10/30 min + dry. Medium-high mess. Intermediate. Nature, detail work. Flag: acrylic stains. _Paint designs or bug-and-ladybug shapes on rocks._

### Papier-Mâché Sea Turtle — 8-10
Supplies: paper plate, crumpled newspaper, flour paste, green paint. 20/40 + 24h dry. High mess. Advanced. Ocean biology. Flag: wheat paste. _Dome the plate with newspaper underneath, paste paper strips, dry, paint a shell and head._

### Pressed-Flower Bookmark — 5-10
Supplies: pressed flowers (from walks), clear contact paper, cardstock, hole punch, ribbon. 7 days prep (press) / 20 activity. Low mess. Intermediate. Nature, patience. _Arrange pressed flowers and seal with contact paper._

### Popsicle-Stick Bug Hotel — 8-10
Supplies: popsicle sticks, tacky glue, twine. 10/40 min + dry. Low mess. Intermediate. Engineering, environmental. _Stack and glue sticks into a log-cabin frame as a backyard bug hotel._

## Year-round themes

### Alphabet / Letter of the Week — 5-7
Supplies: construction paper letter cutout, magazine cutouts or stickers, glue stick. 5/20 min. Low mess. Beginner. Literacy. _Fill the letter with pictures that start with that sound._

### Shape Collage — 2-4
Supplies: pre-cut circles, squares, triangles, glue stick, large paper. 5/20 min. Low mess. Beginner. Geometry, spatial. _Arrange shapes into a house, tree, or animal._

### Color-Mixing Ziploc Squish — 2-4
Supplies: Ziploc bag, two dollops of red/yellow/blue paint, tape. 2/10 min play. Low mess. Beginner. Color science. _Seal paint in a bag and squish to mix._

### Family Portrait — 2-10
Supplies: construction paper, markers, yarn (hair), fabric scraps. 5/25 min. Low mess. Beginner. SEL, family. _Draw each family member; add yarn hair and fabric clothes._

### Friendship Bracelets (simple knot) — 8-10
Supplies: embroidery floss or yarn, tape or clipboard. 5/40 min. Low mess. Intermediate. Fine motor, patterns. _Knot three strands in alternating over-unders._

### Feelings-Faces Paper Plate — 5-7
Supplies: paper plate, markers, brad fastener, paper strip for dial. 10/20 min. Low mess. Beginner. SEL. _Divide the plate into emotions; spin a pointer to today's feeling._

### Community-Helper Paper Puppets — 5-7
Supplies: popsicle sticks, construction paper, markers, glue stick. 5/25 min. Low mess. Beginner. Social studies. _Draw firefighters, doctors, teachers; tape to sticks for play._

### Transportation Collage — 2-4
Supplies: magazine or print-out pictures of vehicles, glue stick, large paper. 5/15 min. Low mess. Beginner. Vocab. _Tear and glue vehicle pictures into a road scene._

### Farm-Animal Paper Plates — 5-7
Supplies: paper plate, construction paper ears/snouts, markers, pom-poms (1"+), glue stick. 5/25 min. Low mess. Beginner. Animal vocab. _Build pig/cow/sheep faces on paper plates._

### Dinosaur Fossil Dig (salt dough) — 5-7
Supplies: salt dough (flour, salt, water), small plastic dino, paintbrush. 20 prep / 25 activity (after dry). Medium mess. Beginner. Paleontology, patience. _Press a dino into dough, dry, chip out with a brush._

### Space Galaxy in a Jar — 8-10
Supplies: clear jar, cotton balls, water, food coloring (blue/purple), glitter. 10/20 min. Medium mess. Intermediate. Science (layering). _Layer dyed cotton and water with glitter for a nebula look._

### Weather Chart Spinner — 5-7
Supplies: paper plate, brad fastener, marker, construction-paper arrow. 10/15 min. Low mess. Beginner. Calendar math. _Divide a plate into sun/cloud/rain/snow; spin the arrow each day._

### Nutrition Plate Collage — 5-7
Supplies: paper plate (quartered), magazine pictures of foods, glue stick. 5/25 min. Low mess. Beginner. Health. _Fill each quadrant with grain, protein, fruit, vegetable images._

### Pet Silhouette Portrait — 5-10
Supplies: construction paper, pencil, scissors, glue stick, frame paper. 10/25 min. Low mess. Intermediate. Family. _Trace and cut a pet's silhouette from memory or photo._

### Zoo-Animal Paper-Bag Puppet — 5-7
Supplies: paper lunch bag, construction paper, markers, glue stick. 5/25 min. Low mess. Beginner. Animal vocab, storytelling. _Glue head pieces onto a folded bag; mouth moves when opened._

### Number-of-the-Day Sticker Page — 5-7
Supplies: paper with large number printed, stickers or dots. 5/15 min. Low mess. Beginner. One-to-one correspondence. _Place that many stickers on the number._
